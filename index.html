About me

Manivanan Sehgar is a Ai Enthusiast. He finished his Sijil Pejalaran Malaysia (SPM) from SM Vokasional Kulim majoring in Computer Programming. After completing SPM, he asked his Guru Ruslina Jusoh on which course should he pursue further in the world of IT and she told him to go for Aritificial Intelligence (Ai). That is the first time he heard the term Ai. He started to learn and explore Ai. Since then he never stop exploring Ai.

He completed his degree in the field of Artificial Intelligence from Multimedia University (MMU) class of 2018. He is specialized on data science in the area of Developing Chatbot using Deep Learning. His main interests are data analytics and sentiment analysis, particularly of social media.

He is also an aspiring entrepreneur who has joined several top start-up business competitions in the world such as Alibaba Global GET Challenge. He has been recognised as top 12 team and received Award of Excellence at Alibaba Get Global Challenge. He also has been recoginesed as IEEE Innovation Nation Malaysia by Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. Last but not least, he is passionate about exploring the world of Ai.
